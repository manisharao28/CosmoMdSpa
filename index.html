About Us

Redefining Beauty Through Expertise and Care

At Cosmo MD Spa, we believe beauty should look effortless and feel empowering. Led by experienced medical professionals, our spa specializes in non-surgical aesthetic treatments designed to enhance your natural features—not mask them. We combine advanced techniques with an artistic eye to deliver results that are subtle, balanced, and tailored just for you. Whether you're looking to smooth fine lines, restore facial volume, or define your contours, our expert team ensures your journey is safe, comfortable, and completely personalized.

Specialities & Qualifications

Dr. Anil Patil, MD is an Interventional Pain Management Physician and Licensed Non-Surgeon Cosmetic Medic in Melville, New York. He is currently licensed to practice medicine in New York. With more than 15 years of experience, the team at Cosmo MD Spa provides personal care and develops a plan that enables you to achieve your cosmetic enhancement goals.
